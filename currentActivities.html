Current Activities

Throughout his career, The Weeknd has been noted for his distinctive voice, his introspective and often explicit lyrics, and his willingness to experiment with different genres and styles. He has since established himself as one of the most innovative and influential artists of his generation and his work continues to be prominent today. In February 2021, Tesfaye headlined the Super Bowl LV halftime show, becoming the first Canadian solo artist to do so. Following his performance, After Hours and the other songs he performed all saw an explosive growth in streams and downloads.

Tesfaye's fifth studio album, Dawn FM, was released on January 7, 2022. The album was a major factor in his latest tour, the After Hours til Dawn Tour, and many elements from his albums After Hours and Dawn FM were incorporated into it. The tour is his seventh concert tour and primarily visits stadiums from across the world. Although the North American leg of the tour recently wrapped up, Tesfaye has plans to also visit South America, Europe, Asia, Africa, the Middle East, and Australia.

As of today, The Weeknd continues to be wildly successful. He has been dubbed the “most popular musician on the planet” by Guinness World Records, among other media organizations that tout his reach and influence. Currently, Tesfaye has over 100 million monthly active listeners on Spotify and is the first artist to reach this momentous milestone—despite only being 33, he has broken countless records and accumulated many accolades and his trajectory will only continue upward.
